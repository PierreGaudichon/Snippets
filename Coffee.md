### Apply, Call
[Call](https://developer.mozilla.org/fr/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Objets_globaux/Function/call), [Apply](https://developer.mozilla.org/fr/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Objets_globaux/Function/apply).

```coffee
  fun.apply @, [a, b]
  fun.call @, a, b
```
